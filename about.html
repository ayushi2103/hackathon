About Us

This is an educational website for this generation for focusing on creating awareness about current female leaders in the tech industry

It was started as an effort to bring about more awareness about women leaders and role models within the tech industry. More often than not, women leaders are sidelined by their male counter parts. It is important for the coming generations to easily identify themselves with such role models and aim for the skies.

This platform showcase powerhouse women each week and invites them for tech talks. It also connects women with community tech leaders near them so the can find out what it takes to be a leader in tech
